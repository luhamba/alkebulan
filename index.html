WE ARE ALKEBULAN

Alkebulan is a contemporary African arts social enterprise that cultivates talent via social hubs and provides local artists with access to global markets though our virtual gallery which enables them to develop their craft, improve their livelihoods and spread our culture.

Ownership

Our organization up-skills young talent through collaboration thereby enabling them to cultivate their creativity and generate revenue.

Our virtual gallery enables local artists to auction their products thereby ensuring that they have access to global markets

We enable African artists to take ownership of their work through royalties via use of block-chain and Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs)
